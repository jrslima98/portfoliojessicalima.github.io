Newspaper

Making an illustration for a newspaper requires some care due to the weight of the paper as well as the use of black / white printing.
With attention to these facts, the illustration would be developed in one color and with few details.
The news chosen was about the pollution of two Portuguese power plants.
I wanted something that would impact and help change habits. After all, the plants exist because there are those who consume energy. In other words, the responsibility rests with all of us.
