REFLEXION

Reflexion is a flipbook animation about myself. This proposal was developed in academia, where we were asked to describe ourselves.
The project started with exploring what I most enjoyed doing - animation and meditation.
It was easy to bring the two together, creating an animation that talked about the process that was going on at the time.

Nowadays we follow standarts imposed by society that lead us to have lives identical to most human beings, but the question is - Could it be that we are becoming prisoners of those standarts, this system?
