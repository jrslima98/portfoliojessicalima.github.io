OFF-Animation

"You are unable to do the right thing!"
"You are lazy!"
"Look at yourself, not even a man / woman can be!
... and you believe in internal television!

Have you tried observe it?

Observe it! Without any hunch.

Are you still your mind?

OFF is an animation that addresses the psychological theme. To be more literal, the voices that don't stop in our head.
Being used to this internal noise, we consider it to be true (we are); leaving the question behind: but is it true?
We perceive ourselves, we perceive others and even life itself through these films that pass through the mind. we are not them, we do have the possibility to believe them.
