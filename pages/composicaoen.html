Composition-Rosa Ramalho

In this project, the objective was to create a composition that portrayed artisan Rosa Ramalho from a predefined theme. That theme was Active. Something active is something with movement, vibrant colors, irregularities, there is manpower, dynamism. We were provided with a photograph of a work by the artisan, in this case the one chosen was "Mulher no Caldeirão" and from there the composition was developed.

Four illustrations were made of "Mulher no Caldeirão. Three in dry pastel and one in acrylic. The typography was made on cardboard. After digitization, the illustrations were superimposed and the typography was placed in order to contribute to the theme.
